About Us

Halo Pro is a website devoted to helping fellow gamers with there experience in the halo series.
throughout our website you can find the names of all the species throughout the halo games and various ways and tactics to deal with them. With these various methods to deal with them you also have some background information on the creature itself. Some of the covenant creatures in this site include: the hunter, jackel, skirmishers, elites, drones, engineers, grunts, brutes, and the mighty scarab.

Why Halo?

We chose halo as our main game to talk about because about 50% or more xbox gamers have seen or played halo before. and most of those gamers have either asked or will ask a question about halo. So this site is all about helping those gamers with questions about halo, and if you have a question that was not answered on this site, post it to the chat so others could help you.
